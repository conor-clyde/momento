Momento is a local-only photo journaling app. Your data stays on your device at all times.
This Privacy Policy explains how Momento handles information when you use the app.

üì∏ Information Accessed

Momento collects and stores only the data you create within the app. All data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

**Data Collected:**
- **Journal entries**: Titles, notes, mood ratings, and timestamps
- **Photos**: Images you capture or select, automatically optimized and compressed for storage efficiency
- **App preferences**: Favorite moments and achievement progress

**Device Features Accessed:**

**Camera Access**
- **Purpose**: To capture photos for your personal journal entries
- **When**: Only when you choose to take a photo using the camera feature
- **Data**: Photos are temporarily captured in memory, then optimized and stored locally on your device

**Photo Library/Media Access**
- **Purpose**: To optionally save photos to your device's camera roll/gallery
- **When**: Only when you choose to save a photo to your camera roll
- **Data**: Photos are copied to your device's standard photo gallery (separate from app's private storage)

**Device Storage**
- **Purpose**: To store your journal entries and optimized photos in the app's private folder
- **Data**: All journal data and photos remain in encrypted, app-private storage on your device

**Network Access**
- **Purpose**: Required for basic app functionality (automatic permission granted by Android)
- **Data**: No network requests are made by the app - this permission enables potential future connectivity but is not currently used

All data is stored exclusively on your device. Momento has no servers, no online accounts, and no cloud storage.

üóÇ How Information Is Used

All data is processed and used exclusively on your device:

**Photo Processing:**
- Photos are automatically optimized (resized to 1200px max width, compressed to 70% quality) to reduce storage usage
- Optimized photos are stored in the app's private documents folder
- Temporary camera files are automatically cleaned up after processing

**Journal Data:**
- Journal entries (titles, notes, mood ratings, timestamps) are stored locally using encrypted device storage
- Achievement progress and app preferences are tracked locally
- Data is used to calculate statistics and display your journaling history within the app

**Optional Camera Roll Saving:**
- When requested, photos can be saved to your device's standard photo gallery
- This creates a separate copy in your camera roll (app's private storage remains unchanged)

Momento never uploads, syncs, backs up, or shares your data externally. All processing happens locally on your device.

üîê Data Security

**Local Storage Security:**
- All data is stored in your device's encrypted storage
- Journal data uses AsyncStorage (encrypted by the operating system)
- Photos are stored in the app's private documents folder
- No data is transmitted over the internet

**Error Logging:**
- Error messages are logged locally for debugging purposes only
- Logs are never transmitted externally and remain on your device
- In production builds, only error logs are retained (info/debug logs are stripped)

**No External Data Handling:**
- No data is stored by the developer
- No data is shared with third parties
- No cloud storage, backups, or synchronization

You have complete control over your journal content. You can delete the app at any time to permanently remove all data.

üåê Third-Party Services

Momento does not use third-party services, analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

**Expo Framework:**
The app is built using Expo, which provides the following device access capabilities:
- **expo-camera**: Camera access for photo capture
- **expo-file-system**: Local file storage management
- **expo-media-library**: Optional camera roll saving
- **expo-image-manipulator**: Photo optimization and compression

All Expo modules operate locally on your device and do not transmit data externally.
